What is Amigurimi?

Amigurumi is the Japanese art of crocheting small stuffed yarn creations

In Japanese“Ami” translates to crocheted or knitted “Kurtumi” translates to wrapping“nuigurumi” translates to stuffed doll. The root of the art is hard to pin down, however, amigurumi dolls began to appear in Japan during the 1970s. It is also known that art has been around for many years before that. There is a lot of creative freedom when making aigurumi's, they can vary in shape, color, and size.
